Recipes

Breakfast

Scrambled Vegetable Egg-White

Serves: 1 • Prep: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (0% or low-fat)
  • 1/4 cup berries
  • 1 tbsp chopped nuts
  • Optional: 1 tsp chia seeds

Directions

  1. Layer yogurt, berries and nuts in a bowl. Serve immediately.

Veggie Egg-White Omelette

Serves: 1 • Prep: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 egg whites
  • Handful of spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Whisk egg whites; cook in a nonstick pan with veggies until set.

Lunch & Dinner

Grilled Salmon & Greens

Serves: 2 • Prep: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 small salmon fillets
  • Mixed greens, cucumber, bell pepper
  • Olive oil, lemon, salt, pepper

Directions

  1. Grill salmon, assemble salad, dress lightly with oil & lemon.

Quinoa & Roasted Veg Bowl

Serves: 2 • Prep: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • Roasted zucchini, eggplant, cherry tomatoes
  • Feta (optional)

Directions

  1. Assemble quinoa and vegetables. Top with small amount of feta.

Desserts & Snacks

Baked Cinnamon Apple (no added sugar)

Serves: 2 • Prep: 10 mins • Bake: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 small apples, cored
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Top apples with cinnamon and walnuts; bake at 350°F (175°C) for ~20 minutes.

Dark Chocolate & Nut Bites

Serves: 6 • Prep: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 oz dark chocolate (70%+)
  • Handful of mixed nuts

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate, mix with nuts, chill until set. Portion into small bites.

All recipes are suggestions. Adjust portions and ingredients to fit personal health plans.
