Tuna Salad In A Lettuce Wrap

Serves: 3 • Prep time: 10 mins • Cook time: None

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna (drain all of the water)
  • 1 tbsp light mayo (or you may use Greek yogurt)
  • Some diced celery
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Large lettuce leaves (such as romaine or butter lettuce)

Directions

  1. Mix the tuna, mayo (or Greek yogurt), diced celery, seasonings, and lemon juice in a bowl till everything is combined.
  2. Scoop your mixtures and place them into the lettuce leaves.
  3. Wrap your lettuce leaves a bit so the mixture stays in place.
  4. You may add any extra vegetables as toppings if you like.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
